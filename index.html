About Me

I am a full stack software developer with 10 years of professional experience as a science teacher and educator. After encouraging my former students to learn to code, I quickly got hooked on teaching myself Python and Javascript, which ultimately led me to enroll in Prime Digital Academy's full stack software engineering program in July 2018. With the newfound skills I've developed by learning to apply technologies including React, Redux, Javascript, HTML, CSS3, Node.js, and PostgreSQL to authentic problems, I look forward to bringing my love of learning, clear style of communication, and passion for problem solving to a new role as a software developer in the Twin Cities metro area.

In my spare time I enjoy making music, delicious food, and exploring the great outdoors.
